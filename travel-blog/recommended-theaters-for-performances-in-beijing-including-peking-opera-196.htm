Recommended Theaters for Performances in Beijing, Including Peking Opera

  • Home
  • Travel Blog
  • Blog Details

Beijing, the capital of China, is not only the political and cultural heart of the country, but also a hub for traditional and contemporary performances. One of the most iconic forms of Chinese theater that visitors often seek to experience in Beijing is Peking Opera (Jingju), a classical performance art that blends singing, acting, martial arts, and acrobatics. However, the city offers much more than just Peking Opera, with a wide range of performances that cater to different tastes and interests. Whether you're a fan of traditional Chinese theater or contemporary art, Beijing has something for everyone.

In this article, we’ll explore the best theaters in Beijing where you can catch some of the most remarkable performances, including Peking Opera, acrobatic shows, and more. These venues are known not only for their impressive productions but also for their historical significance and cultural importance in Beijing's artistic landscape.

1. Liyuan Theatre (Li Yuan Xiqu Guan)

Located in the heart of Beijing, Liyuan Theatre is one of the most famous venues for Peking Opera performances. It has earned a reputation as one of the top spots for experiencing this classic Chinese art form. Peking Opera at Liyuan Theatre is typically performed by some of China’s most renowned Peking Opera artists, and the shows are known for their authenticity and excellence.

Why Liyuan Theatre is Special:

  • Traditional Peking Opera Performances: This theater offers high-quality performances of traditional Peking Opera, with performances that include everything from elaborate costumes to traditional makeup and acrobatic movements.
  • Excellent acoustics and intimate setting: The theater has been designed with optimal acoustics, ensuring that every note of the music and every word of the performance reaches the audience clearly. The relatively small size of the venue also allows for an intimate experience, making it a perfect place to enjoy the full grandeur of Peking Opera.
  • Tourist-friendly Shows: Liyuan Theatre is popular with international tourists because the performances are often accompanied by English subtitles. This makes it an ideal place for those who are not familiar with the intricate plots and poetic dialogue of Peking Opera.

Recommended Performance:

The theater’s most famous show is "Farewell My Concubine" (Chou Xiang Xian), a tragic tale of loyalty, love, and war, performed by some of the finest artists in the field. This performance is often considered one of the highlights of any visit to Liyuan Theatre.

2. National Centre for the Performing Arts (NCPA)

Known as the "Giant Egg" due to its unique and modern architectural design, the National Centre for the Performing Arts (NCPA) is one of the most prestigious and famous performance venues in Beijing. While it is known for its large-scale productions of international opera, ballet, and symphonies, the NCPA also hosts Peking Opera performances and a wide range of other traditional Chinese performances.

Why NCPA is Special:

  • Stunning Architecture: The NCPA's striking "egg-shaped" building, surrounded by a reflecting pool, is one of the most recognizable landmarks in Beijing. The venue's architectural beauty itself is a work of art and adds to the overall experience of attending a performance here.
  • World-Class Productions: NCPA regularly hosts high-caliber international performances and has a reputation for presenting productions that are at the cutting edge of art and technology. The venue features not only Peking Opera but also world-class symphonies, operas, and ballets.
  • Cultural Fusion: In addition to traditional Chinese theater, NCPA also features performances that blend Chinese cultural traditions with global performing arts. The center often hosts performances that integrate Chinese operatic forms with Western classical styles, creating a unique fusion of artistic cultures.

Recommended Performance:

For those interested in a fusion of traditional and contemporary, the Peking Opera performances at NCPA are often infused with modern stage design and technology, offering a new take on this centuries-old art form. Additionally, NCPA is renowned for its international opera and classical music events.

3. Chang'an Grand Theatre (Chang'an Dasha)

The Chang'an Grand Theatre is another iconic venue in Beijing, known for its high-level performances of Peking Opera, Chinese traditional dance, and acrobatics. This theater hosts some of the most famous Peking Opera productions and features a diverse range of performances from across China.

Why Chang'an Grand Theatre is Special:

  • Great Location: Situated on Chang'an Street, one of Beijing’s main thoroughfares, the theater is easily accessible and often visited by both locals and tourists alike.
  • Elegant Design: The theater itself is a sight to behold, with its beautifully designed interiors and large performance space that allows for grand productions. The stage is equipped with advanced lighting and sound systems, ensuring that every performance is presented with stunning visual and auditory effects.
  • Wide Range of Performances: While the Chang'an Grand Theatre is particularly well known for its Peking Opera, it also hosts a variety of other performances, including traditional Chinese dance, acrobatics, and even musical theater. This makes it a great place to experience a variety of Chinese performing arts.

Recommended Performance:

The Red Lantern (Hong Deng Ji) is one of the most famous Peking Opera performances at the Chang'an Grand Theatre. The story centers around the dramatic tale of a woman’s sacrifices for her family, and it is widely regarded as one of the most touching productions in Peking Opera.

4. Beijing Acrobatic Theatre

While Beijing is most famous for its Peking Opera, it is also home to some of the world’s best acrobatic shows, and the Beijing Acrobatic Theatre is the place to experience these jaw-dropping performances. Known for its skillful performers and breathtaking routines, the Beijing Acrobatic Theatre showcases some of the most impressive acts in Chinese acrobatics.

Why Beijing Acrobatic Theatre is Special:

  • World-Class Acrobatics: The Beijing Acrobatic Theatre is home to some of the finest acrobatic performers in China. The performances typically include acts such as contortionism, juggling, aerial performances, and human pyramids, all executed with incredible skill and precision.
  • Innovative Shows: While traditional Chinese acrobatic performances remain a staple, the Beijing Acrobatic Theatre is known for blending modern technology and set design into its performances, creating a unique and innovative experience for audiences.
  • Family-Friendly: The Beijing Acrobatic Theatre’s shows are particularly popular with families and children, as the performances are visually stunning and full of exciting action. The entire family can enjoy a thrilling and unforgettable experience here.

Recommended Performance:

The Golden Mask Dynasty is one of the most famous acrobatic performances at the Beijing Acrobatic Theatre. This production tells the story of the ancient Chinese imperial court with elaborate acrobatic routines, breathtaking stunts, and stunning visual effects.

5. Mei Lanfang Theatre

Named after the legendary Peking Opera master Mei Lanfang, the Mei Lanfang Theatre is dedicated to preserving and promoting Peking Opera and traditional Chinese theater. Located in the Xicheng district, this theater is particularly special for those who are interested in the history and artistry of Peking Opera.

Why Mei Lanfang Theatre is Special:

  • A Tribute to Mei Lanfang: Mei Lanfang was one of the most famous Peking Opera performers of the 20th century, known for his portrayal of female roles (dan) in Peking Opera. The theater named after him is a tribute to his legacy and offers visitors an opportunity to experience the Peking Opera as he might have performed it.
  • Intimate Setting: The Mei Lanfang Theatre is smaller than some of the other venues in Beijing, which gives the performances a more intimate and personal feel. Visitors can truly immerse themselves in the performance, whether it's a classic Peking Opera or a modern reinterpretation of the traditional form.
  • Focus on Tradition: Unlike larger venues that often feature contemporary adaptations, the Mei Lanfang Theatre primarily focuses on preserving the traditional aspects of Peking Opera, making it a must-visit for anyone looking to experience the purest form of this art.

Recommended Performance:

At Mei Lanfang Theatre, you can watch a performance of "The Drunken Concubine" (Gui Fei Zui Jiu), one of the most famous and beloved works in the Peking Opera repertoire. This performance features a beautiful, tragic story, with extraordinary singing and acting.

6. National Peking Opera Company Theatre

The National Peking Opera Company Theatre is a well-respected venue that hosts some of the best Peking Opera performances in Beijing. The performances here are often by the National Peking Opera Company, which is considered one of the top Peking Opera troupes in China.

Why National Peking Opera Company Theatre is Special:

  • Renowned Peking Opera Troupe: As one of the leading companies in the country, the National Peking Opera Company consistently delivers world-class performances of classic and modern Peking Opera works.
  • Cultural Significance: The National Peking Opera Company Theatre is a key venue in preserving the traditions of Peking Opera, and it often features performances that reflect China’s rich cultural heritage and historical themes.

Recommended Performance:

The National Peking Opera Company Theatre often stages classic operas such as "The Legend of the White Snake" (Bai She Zhuan), a legendary love story with supernatural elements that has captivated audiences for centuries.

Beijing is a city rich in cultural heritage and artistic expression, and its theaters reflect the diversity and depth of Chinese performing arts. Whether you're interested in experiencing a traditional Peking Opera performance, a thrilling acrobatic show, or a modern theatrical production, Beijing offers a wealth of options for every type of theatergoer. Make sure to visit some of the city’s top theaters and enjoy the performances that showcase the artistic brilliance of this vibrant city.
